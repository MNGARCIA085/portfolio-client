A LITTLE ABOUT ME

I was born in Montevideo, Uruguay but I live my childhood and adolescence in Carmelo. If someone ask me where I'm from I say Carmelo.
When I turned 18 I went back to Montevideo to study, and I was able to graduate as an electrical engineer. I had the opportunity to study thanks to my family, who always supported me.
I like software development and IA because they allow you to work in very different areas; you can develop management software for an hospital, an app that suggests the best places to eat in the area, an app that warns of a fire approaching and it's even possible to create art. Everything is possible.
I like sports,I do cycling and swimming.
I started with death note and it is without a doubt one of my 5 favorite series of all time.
I am very curious about regenerative medicine and I would like to work in this field, to be able to contribute with something that helps improve people's quality of life.
